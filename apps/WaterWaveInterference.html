Theory & Formulas

1. Path Difference ($\Delta$)

The path difference is the difference in distance from the two sources ($S_1, S_2$) to a specific point ($P$).

$$\Delta = |S_1P - S_2P|$$

2. Phase Difference of waves arriving at a point

The phase difference between waves arriving at a point depends on path difference and phase difference of the sources.

3. Conditions for Interference

When sources are in phase ($\phi_0 = 0^\circ$):

  • Constructive: $\Delta = n\lambda$
  • Destructive: $\Delta = (n + 0.5)\lambda$
