Theory of Electromagnetic Induction

1. Generator vs Motor Operation

  • Motor Mode: An external constant current source feeds current into the coil. The magnetic field exerts Lorentz forces on the wire loop, creating torque ($\tau = N I B A \sin\theta$) which drives rotation. As the motor speeds up, it generates an opposing Back EMF.
  • Generator Mode: Mechanical energy rotates the coil. By Faraday's Law, changing flux induces an EMF ($\varepsilon = -d\Phi/dt$). The induced current flows backwards (Lenz's Law) and interacts with the magnetic field to produce an opposing magnetic drag force (Fleming's Left-Hand Rule).
  • Universal Motor (AC Motor): The stator uses electromagnets powered by the same AC source in series with the armature. The magnetic field reverses in perfect sync with the armature current, ensuring the Lorentz Force ($\vec{F} = \vec{I} \times \vec{B}$) continues to push in the same direction!
Motor Dynamics (Constant Current): $$I = I_{\text{applied}}$$ $$P_m = \tau \cdot \omega$$ $$\tau = N I B A \sin(\theta)$$ $$\varepsilon_{\text{back}} = N B A \omega \sin(\theta)$$

2. Commutator Mechanics

  • Split-Ring Commutator (DC): Reverses current polarity every half rotation ($180^\circ$). This mechanical switching guarantees the torque pushes in a single, unidirectional continuous rotation.
  • Slip Rings (AC): Maintains continuous contacts. In a generator, it directly outputs the raw alternating sinusoidal current.

1. 發電機與電動機原理

  • 電動機模式： 外接電源為線圈注入電流。磁場對通電導線產生磁力，產生電磁轉矩 ($\tau = N I B A \sin\theta$) 驅動線圈旋轉。當線圈旋轉時，會產生反電動勢 (Back EMF)。
  • 發電機模式： 利用機械能旋轉線圈。根據法拉第定律，磁通量變化產生感應電動勢 ($\varepsilon = -d\Phi/dt$)。根據楞次定律，感應電流將反向流動，並受到磁場作用產生阻礙線圈旋轉的磁阻力 (以弗萊明左手定律 $\vec{F} = \vec{I} \times \vec{B}$ 判斷)。
  • 通用電動機 (交流摩打)： 定子使用與交流電源串聯的電磁鐵。當交流電反向時，磁場與電樞電流同時反向，使洛倫茲力始終維持單向推進！
電動機動力學公式 (恆定電流)： $$I = I_{\text{外加}}$$ $$\tau = N I B A \sin(\theta)$$ $$\varepsilon_{\text{反}} = N B A \omega \sin(\theta)$$

2. 整流機制之別

  • 換向器（兩個半環） (直流)： 每半轉 ($180^\circ$) 自動切換線圈電流極性。這確保線圈能持續獲得單向的推進轉矩，實現連續旋轉。
  • 匯電環 (交流)： 永久連接各端。在發電機中，它能將線圈內產生的原始交流電直接輸出。
